FEATURES

VSCarbon was designed and made by an group of young changemakers so as to save our planet , create an impact , reduce the carbon footprint and have fun. So VSCarbon offers it all!
  • INFORMING
  • MEASURING
  • TAKING ACTION

For Scientists

The application is a strong tool to be used by the scientists to identify the sources of carbon emission and carbon footprint. The data collected will be well visualized so as to be used by the scientists to take the right actions and have a strong database available for scientific purpose.

For Individuals

Individuals will be in touch with their daily evolution. In fact they will get to know wether they are reducing or no their carbon footprint by doing the different challenges and by creating a positive impact in a daily basis.

Measure your carbon footprint is now possible

After you've filled the form. You are now ready to enter the amazing world of challenges. But isnt perfect to be able to measure your impact ? And what about your carbon footprint ? Are you up for the challenge ?

Carbon Footprint

We often want to calculate our carbon footprint but we dont know how to do so or so often , we find it so hard. Our application will help you calculate your carbon footprint and get a clear insight of your situation and the level or your carbon footprint. Isn't so cool ?

Impact

You did calculate the carbon footprint. We really want to measure our impact. But how possible we can measure this impact! Our application offers you the possibility to measure your impact and the impact you create by doing an action and shows you how much you actually did impact positively by doing the different challenges.

The community & the league

The exciting part is the community of changemakers created. The challenges will be displayed and custumised in a way YOU can actually do them. A challenge is an easy action with levels. Why creating a community ? You can , in fact , invite your friends to do the challenges and get the diamonds and you can even create a challenge within your community in the same city. The league is the surpise. There will be an international league where you can actually share your impact internationnally , participate in challenges and become the best challenger and the best impacter in the fight against Carbon.

The challenges

The challenges will be an easy to do challenges. In fact these challenges will be activities fun and easy to do. The challenges are divided into categories while taking in consideration the carbon footprint of the user , in a way to invite everyone to take actions and encourage them to create a positive impact in a daily basis with no cpmplications. The challenges are given and offers the opportunity to users to upload pictures and videos about their work. The user can also have the opportunity to create his own challenge and invite other changemakers to do it too.

Decision Makers

Having a strong database of users and changermakers will help decision makers take the right decision when it comes to taking global actions , creating and implementing projects in the differents regions. Decisions makers will also be included in the process of creating an impact when it comes to reducing the carbon footprint locally.

Local Impact

The local impact is so important. The user is able to create an impact locally. In fact , the application will help the collaboration between the different citizen locally.Creating an impact and doing a challenge can vary from an action being done locally to an action being done in whole street , city or even the whole region.

Dear user , with VSCarbon you can actually create an impact and reduce your carbon footprint while having fun a the same time
